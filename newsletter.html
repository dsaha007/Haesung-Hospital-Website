"Harmony in Healthcare: Haesung Hospital's Collaborative Military Operation"

Date & Time: January 20, 2023, 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Location: Earthquake Site
Report by: Alex Johnson

Step into the realm of solidarity and service with Haesung Hospital's Collaborative Military Operation event! This event celebrates the extraordinary joint efforts of Haesung Hospital's medical experts and the military in a life-saving mission during a catastrophic earthquake. Explore the tales of bravery, resilience, and the spirit of teamwork as these heroes worked tirelessly to rescue and provide critical medical care to the earthquake victims. Experience firsthand the incredible narratives of hope, survival, and the unwavering commitment to serving humanity during times of crisis.
