About Haesung

Haesung Hospital is a top-tier hospital renowned for its world-class healthcare facilities. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and cutting-edge technology, we offer a wide range of specialized services:

  • Advanced Diagnostic Imaging
  • Robotic Surgery
  • Intensive Care Units (ICUs)
  • Rehabilitation Center
  • Specialized Pediatric Care
  • Oncology Center
  • Cardiac Catheterization Lab
  • Transplant Services
  • Women's Health Services
  • Mental Health & Wellness Programs

Our hospital, housing 5000 beds and backed by a dedicated team of 800 full-time staff, is committed to delivering exceptional care to our patients. We pride ourselves on offering world-class medical services at affordable prices, aligning with our mission of "Serving Mankind." As the largest hospital chain in South Korea, we have expanded our footprint to 20 cities across 4 countries, continually striving for excellence in healthcare.
